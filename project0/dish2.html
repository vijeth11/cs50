Onion Rings

Wow! Onion rings is definitely one snack that is eaten for its crispy and crunchy taste. Theyâ€™re just super deep fried spicy rings, easy to prepare, cheap as well as delicious. Onion rings is a popular side dish commonly found in the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, Australia and some parts of Asia. Generally making of this recipe consist of a cross-sectional ring onion which is dipped in bread crumbs, spicy flour and deep fried until crisp and crunchy. This makes a fantastic presentation through a variety of sizes of onions. Onion rings can be eaten on its own or else dipped into ketchup or spicy sauces.

Ingridients:

Items Quantity
Bread crumbs 1 cup
Chilli powder to taste
Cornflour 3 tbsp
Large onion 1 no
Salt to taste
All-purpose flour (maida) 1 cup
Oil for deep frying
Soda pinch
Water as required

Recipie:

  • Cut onions into rings and keep a side.
  • In a bowl add all purpose flour, corn flour, soda, salt, chilli powder, bread crumbs and mix with water to a thick batter.
  • Now take onion rings, coat with the dry flour and dip in the flour mixture and coat with bread crumbs.
  • Drop the slowly in hot oil and deep fry until golden brown colour.
  • Serve this hot with chili garlic sauce.
